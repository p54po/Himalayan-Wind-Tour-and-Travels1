Village Tour

Experience authentic rural life just outside Darjeeling as you wander through mist‑shrouded tea gardens and quaint mountain villages. Meet warm local families, taste freshly brewed cha and homemade snacks, and watch traditional handicrafts being made by expert hands. End your day atop a nearby ridge, soaking in panoramic Himalayan vistas as village lights twinkle below.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Pickup from Bagdogra/NJP to Darjeeling.
  • Day 2: Darjeeling to takdah, Lamahatta, Peshok tea garden.
  • Day 3: Drop to NJP/Airport.
