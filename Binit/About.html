About Us

Himalayan Wind Tour & Travels is your trusted travel companion for exploring the breathtaking regions of Darjeeling, Sikkim, Mirik, Kalingpoong and others.

Our mission is to offer unique, authentic, and immersive experiences for travelers of all kinds — whether it's serene mountain retreats, cultural explorations, or thrilling adventures.

What We Offer

Mountain Tours

Explore the majestic Himalayan landscapes with guided experiences and cozy stays.

Cultural Tours

Immerse yourself in rich traditions, local festivals, and heritage walks.

Honeymoon Specials

Romantic destinations with personalized experiences for unforgettable memories.

Adventure Packages

Trekking, rafting, paragliding and more — for the thrill-seekers and explorers.

Wildlife Safaris

Discover rare flora and fauna across national parks and sanctuaries.

River Cruises
