Explore Our Destinations

Discover the beauty and charm of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, Sikkim, and beyond. Our carefully curated destinations are perfect for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and those wanting to experience the Himalayan culture. Let us guide you through the picturesque landscapes and hidden gems of the region.

Darjeeling

Known for its stunning tea gardens and majestic views of the Kanchenjunga peak, Darjeeling is a perfect blend of colonial charm and Himalayan beauty. A place where serenity meets adventure.

Kalimpong

Kalimpong offers breathtaking views, serene monasteries, and a rich cultural heritage. A peaceful escape into nature and spirituality. Perfect for those looking for quiet exploration.

Sikkim

From the mesmerizing landscapes of Tsomgo Lake to the peaceful monasteries and majestic mountains, Sikkim is an adventurer's paradise. Explore remote villages, high-altitude treks, and more.

Darjeeling Zoo

Explore the famous Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park to witness the rich biodiversity and the rare red panda in the lap of the Himalayas. A must-visit for wildlife enthusiasts.

Tsomgo Lake

Located at an altitude of 12,400 feet, Tsomgo Lake offers stunning views and a spiritual vibe. Known for its frozen water during winters, it's one of the most picturesque spots in Sikkim.

Khangchendzonga National Park

This UNESCO World Heritage site is home to diverse wildlife, alpine meadows, glaciers, and towering mountains, including the third-highest peak in the world—Kangchenjunga. Perfect for trekkers and nature lovers.
