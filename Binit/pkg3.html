Mixed sightseeing

3 Nights / 4 Days

Escape to the misty hills of Darjeeling — a charming hill station known for its panoramic mountain views, tea plantations, and the iconic toy train ride. This 3 Nights / 4 Days package offers the perfect blend of sightseeing, relaxation, and Mixed Sightseeing.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Day 1: Pickup from Bagdogra/NJP .

  • Day 3:Visit Ghoom Monastery and the Japanese Peace Pagoda Explore the Rock Garden and nearby waterfall (e.g. Chunnu Summer Falls)
